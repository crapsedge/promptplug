Parties: This agreement is between the enrolling contractor (“Contractor”) and Omaha Restoration™ (“Platform”), operated by Murrays Empire LLC (“Company”).

1) Referral Model: Platform may route requests for emergency water damage / flood cleanup and related restoration services. Contractor agrees to pay a $150.00 referral fee for every Job Start originating from the Platform.

2) “Job Start” Defined: “Job Start” means the customer signs, authorizes, or otherwise approves paid work, including emergency authorization, work authorization, mitigation authorization, or a signed estimate, and Contractor begins or schedules work.

3) Payment Timing: Referral fees are due within five (5) business days of Job Start. Platform will invoice Contractor by email, and Contractor agrees to pay electronically upon receipt.

4) Proof & Disputes: Contractor agrees Platform may rely on reasonable proof of Job Start, including call logs, texts/emails, customer confirmation, recorded call summary notes, or Contractor’s own confirmation. Any dispute must be submitted in writing within three (3) business days of the invoice; otherwise the invoice is deemed accepted.

5) No Guarantees / No Exclusivity: Platform does not guarantee any volume, number of calls, or number of Job Starts. Unless separately agreed in writing, this agreement is non-exclusive and Contractor may accept work from any other source.

6) Termination: Either party may terminate participation at any time by written notice. Termination does not affect fees owed for Job Starts that occurred prior to termination.

7) Liability Cap: Platform’s total liability for any claim is limited to $100.00.

8) Independent Contractor / No Agency: Contractor is an independent business and not an employee, agent, joint venturer, or partner of Platform/Company. Contractor controls all work performance, pricing, licensing, insurance, permits, safety standards, and customer communications.

9) Indemnification: Contractor agrees to defend, indemnify, and hold harmless Platform/Company from any claims, damages, disputes, injuries, losses, or costs arising from Contractor’s services, workmanship, acts/omissions, or customer disputes.

10) Governing Law: This agreement is governed by the laws of the state where the work is performed, unless otherwise required by law.

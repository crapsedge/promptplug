1) Platform Role / No Guarantee: Platform is a referral and lead-generation service only. Platform makes no guarantees regarding the quality, safety, legality, timing, pricing, or outcome of Contractor’s work. Contractor is solely responsible for all services performed.

2) Referral Model: Contractor agrees to pay a $150 referral fee for each Job Start.

3) Job Start Defined: A Job Start occurs when the customer authorizes paid work and Contractor begins or schedules services.

4) Reporting Requirement: Contractor must notify Platform within twenty-four (24) hours if a referred lead does not result in a Job Start. Failure to notify constitutes acceptance that a Job Start occurred and the referral fee becomes immediately due.

5) Payment Terms: Referral fees are due within five (5) business days of Job Start.

6) Independent Contractor / No Partnership: Contractor is an independent business and not an employee, partner, agent, joint venturer, or representative of Platform. Contractor is responsible for its own taxes, tools, labor, insurance, and workers’ compensation.

7) Credential & Insurance Requirements: Contractor represents that it maintains all licenses, permits, and insurance required to legally and safely perform its services, including general liability insurance where applicable.

8) Proof Upon Request: Contractor agrees to provide proof of credentials or insurance upon request. Failure to do so may result in suspension or termination of referrals.

9) High-Risk Work: For water mitigation, restoration, tree removal, or storm-related work, active insurance is a condition of receiving referrals.

10) No Control Over Work: Platform does not control pricing, scheduling, methods, labor, materials, or customer communications.

11) Customer Relationship: Any agreement for services exists solely between Contractor and the customer. Platform is not a party to such agreements.

12) Indemnification: Contractor agrees to indemnify, defend, and hold harmless Platform and Company from any claims, damages, or liabilities arising from Contractor’s work.

13) Limitation of Liability: Platform’s total liability shall not exceed referral fees paid during the preceding three (3) months.

14) Dispute Resolution / Arbitration: Any dispute arising from this Agreement shall be resolved by binding arbitration in Nebraska under AAA rules, and not in court, except either party may bring an individual claim in small claims court if eligible.

15) Governing Law: This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Nebraska.

16) Entire Agreement: This document constitutes the entire agreement between the parties.

5) Referral Fee; Job Start Definition. Contractor agrees to pay a referral fee of $150 per Job Start for each customer referral originating from the Platform. No Job Start = no referral fee.

6) Payment Timing. Referral fees are due within 5 business days of Job Start unless otherwise agreed in writing. Acceptable payment methods may include ACH, check, or other methods provided by Platform.

1. Referral & Routing Services

Omaha Restoration™ is a digital routing service operated by Murrays Empire LLC. This platform connects property owners with third-party, independent restoration contractors. We are not a licensed contractor and do not perform water mitigation, cleanup, or construction work.

2. Data Collection Disclosure

Data submitted via our intake forms is transmitted to Murrays Empire LLC for lead routing purposes. By submitting a request, you agree that your information may be shared with up to two (2) independent contractors in our network to facilitate your service request.

3. Revenue & Compensation

Omaha Restoration™ may receive a referral fee from independent contractors when a connection is successfully made or a job is initiated. This fee is paid by the contractor and does not increase the cost of your service.

4. No Guarantees of Service

While we only route to vetted local experts, Omaha Restoration™ does not guarantee contractor availability, response times, or specific project outcomes. Any contract for physical work is strictly between the homeowner and the independent contractor.

5. Homeowner Responsibility

It is the user's responsibility to confirm the following before physical work begins:

  • Contractor possesses active General Liability insurance.
  • Contractor holds necessary local or state licenses for the specific work type.
  • A written work authorization or estimate has been signed.

6. Emergency Life-Safety

If you are in immediate physical danger or facing a life-safety emergency, dial 911 or your local emergency services immediately. Omaha Restoration™ is not an emergency dispatcher for first responders.

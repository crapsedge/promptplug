Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2026

This policy explains how Murray’s Empire LLC handles information. We believe in being private and simple.

1. No Data Collection

On this website, we do not ask for or save your name, your email, your phone number, or your address. You can browse our site without telling us who you are.

2. Routing Links

Our website acts as a "digital doorway." When you click a link to find a restoration professional, you are leaving our site. The company you visit next might ask for your info, but we never see it and we never keep it.

3. Automated Systems

We use automated tools to keep our website running. These tools do not track your personal identity. We comply with the Nebraska Data Privacy Act (NDPA) by simply not collecting your data in the first place.

4. AI Disclosure

We use AI technology to help organize our website and make sure the links we provide are helpful to you.

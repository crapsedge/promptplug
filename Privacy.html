Privacy Policy

Updated: April 2026
Data Privacy Standard Omaha Restoration™ is committed to handling your data with transparency. We collect only what is necessary to connect you with emergency restoration professionals.

1. Information We Collect

When you use our Emergency Intake or Contractor Enrollment forms, we collect information including your name, phone number, email address, and service location. This data is used solely to facilitate the referral process.

2. How Data is Shared

As a referral platform, we transmit your contact details to independent third-party contractors in our network. By submitting a request, you authorize Murrays Empire LLC to share this information so a service provider can contact you.

3. Technical Logging

We use basic technical logging (IP addresses, browser type, and timestamps) for security, lead verification, and to prevent fraudulent submissions. This helps us maintain a reliable "Lead Log" for business records.

4. Analytics & Tracking

We use Google Analytics and Google Ads tracking to understand how users find our site. This data is used to optimize our marketing and does not allow us to see your personal private files or off-site activity.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to independent contractors. Once you leave omaharestoration.store, we are no longer responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. Please review their individual policies.

6. Data Ownership

Omaha Restoration™ is a brand owned and operated by Murrays Empire LLC, a Nebraska limited liability company. All data collected is managed under this parent entity.

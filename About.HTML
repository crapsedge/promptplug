About Omaha Restoration™

Referrals, routing, and resources — not a contractor.

Omaha Restoration™ is a referral / routing platform operated by Murrays Empire LLC. We do not perform restoration services, emergency response, inspections, estimates, or repairs.

1. What This Site Is

Omaha Restoration™ helps visitors get connected with independent third-party restoration contractors serving the Omaha area. If you submit a request on this site, the information is used for referral/routing purposes and may be shared with one or more independent contractors so they can follow up directly.

2. What This Site Is NOT

This website is not a restoration company, and it does not dispatch technicians. We do not provide on-site work, and we do not control contractor pricing, availability, timing, or outcomes.

  • No emergency response or 24/7 dispatch.
  • No estimates, inspections, remediation, or repairs.
  • No contractor supervision, licensing, or warranties.

3. How the Connection Works

Omaha Restoration™ is designed to be simple. You can submit a request in under a minute. After submission, your request may be routed to one or more independent contractors based on service type and coverage area. Contractors are responsible for confirming availability, pricing, and scope directly with you.

4. Compensation Disclosure

Murrays Empire LLC may receive compensation from contractors or third parties in connection with referrals routed through this platform. Compensation does not increase the price you pay a contractor. See our Disclosures for details.

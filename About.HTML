About Omaha Restoration™

Routing, referrals, and resources — not a contractor.

Omaha Restoration™ is a referral / routing platform operated by Murrays Empire LLC. We do not perform restoration services, emergency response, inspections, estimates, or repairs.

1. What This Site Is

Omaha Restoration™ exists to help visitors quickly reach third-party restoration resources. When you click outbound links on this website, you may be directed to independent companies, directories, or networks that can connect you with service providers.

2. What This Site Is NOT

This website is not a restoration company, and it does not dispatch technicians. We do not provide on-site work, and we do not control contractor pricing, availability, timing, or outcomes.

  • No emergency response or 24/7 dispatch.
  • No estimates, inspections, remediation, or repairs.
  • No contractor supervision, licensing, or warranties.

3. How We Stay Simple

Omaha Restoration™ is designed to be lightweight and privacy-respecting. The homepage routing button is meant to help visitors move forward without needing to create an account, call anyone, or submit information on this site.

4. Compensation Disclosure

Some outbound links may be affiliate or referral links. If you engage a third-party after clicking, Murrays Empire LLC may receive compensation at no additional cost to you. See our Disclosures for details.

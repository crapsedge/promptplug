Contractor Lead Assignment

This page is provided to contractors who have received a project referral through the Omaha Restoration™ network. Omaha Restoration operates as a referral and routing platform connecting property owners with independent restoration service providers in the Omaha, Nebraska region.

The payment on this page applies to the Job Start Referral Fee associated with a referred project that has begun.

Payment Trigger: The Job Start Referral Fee becomes due when the referred project begins and the contractor proceeds with the work.

Job Start Referral Fee

A one-time referral fee applies to referred projects that originate from the Omaha Restoration network and proceed to active work.

Omaha Restoration Lead Referral Fee (Job Start)

This payment confirms participation in the Omaha Restoration referral network for the assigned project.

Completion Referral Fee

In addition to the Job Start Referral Fee collected on this page, a completion-based referral fee equal to ten percent (10%) of the final project value may apply to work originating from an Omaha Restoration referral.

Following completion of the referred project, a separate payment request reflecting the applicable completion referral fee will be issued based on the final project value reported by the contractor.
